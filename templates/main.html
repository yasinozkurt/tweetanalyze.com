Sentiment analysis is basically a text analysis and aims to determine the class (positive, negative and neutral) that the given text wants to express emotionally. Sentiment analysis is used to understand how customers evaluate the services and products offered by companies. This is usually achieved by analyzing customer feedback. By automatically analyzing customer feedback from various places such as survey responses and social media conversations, brands can track what their customers think about the product or service. Besides, it can customize its products/services to meet customers' needs. Different methods are used to determine what type of emotion a comment/thought contains.
