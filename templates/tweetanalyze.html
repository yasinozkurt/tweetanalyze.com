Sentimental Analysis for Tweets?

Sentiment analysis, also known as opinion mining or emotion AI,is the process of analyzing online pieces of writing to determine the emotional tone they carry, whether they’re positive, negative, or neutral. Sentiment analysis helps to find the author’s attitude towards a topic. Sentiment analysis tools categorize pieces of writing as positive, neutral, or negative.
